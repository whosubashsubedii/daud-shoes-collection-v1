WHO ARE WE?

Daud is a contemporary shoe company founded with the mission of blending modern aesthetics with traditional craftsmanship. Originating from a vision to create footwear that honors cultural heritage while embracing innovative design, Daud was established by a team of designers and researchers passionate about sustainable fashion. The company's purpose is to deliver high-quality, comfortable shoes that reflect individuality and authenticity, while minimizing environmental impact. Extensive research went into material sourcing, biomechanical performance, and ergonomic design, drawing on both modern textile technologies and ancestral shoemaking techniques. By collaborating with artisans and incorporating feedback from wearers across diverse lifestyles, Daud ensures each product is a fusion of durability, style, and purpose.
